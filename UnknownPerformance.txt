[DescBack/Description]
Absurdly simplifies game sprites and world elements. Flat Silhouette keeps shapes but strips textures into solid average colors. Flat Blocks turns everything into literal colored rectangles.=【千万别开，一点都不要开】极度简化游戏贴图与世界元素。“平面剪影”保留形状，但会将纹理替换为单一平均色。“平面色块”则把所有物体变为纯粹的彩色矩形。【不知道 Playground 是啥。开了辣眼睛，开了进游戏里再关闭要重启游戏】
Completely disables small decorative grass, vines, foliage, debris, and blood splatters. Gives the world a clean, minimalist playground style and improves CPU/GPU performance.=完全禁用小型装饰草、藤蔓、植被、碎片和血迹。让世界呈现干净、极简的 Playground 风格，同时提升 CPU/GPU 性能。
Controls how frequently Line Of Sight checks are performed for AI. Higher intervals significantly improve FPS in crowded areas, with minor visual delay.=控制 AI 执行视线检测的频率。间隔越高，越能显著提升拥挤区域的帧率，但会伴随轻微的视觉延迟。
Limits the maximum active splatter, blood, and spark particles on the screen. Lower limits prevent CPU and GPU bottlenecking during heavy combat.=限制屏幕上同时存在的飞溅、血迹和火花粒子最大数量。降低上限可避免激烈战斗中的 CPU 与 GPU 瓶颈。
Adjusts the solver iteration accuracy for 2D physics. Lower iterations reduce CPU overhead from collision solving without affecting normal gameplay.=调整 2D 物理求解器的迭代精度。降低迭代次数可减少碰撞求解带来的 CPU 开销，且不影响正常游戏体验。
Optimizes Unity Garbage Collection time slices to keep gameplay completely micro-stutter free, combined with automated memory sweeping.=优化 Unity 垃圾回收的时间片分配，结合自动内存清扫，彻底消除游戏中的微卡顿。
